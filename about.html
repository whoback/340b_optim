    About the 340B Optimizer

    The 340B Optimizer is a 340B software offering that is owned and created by Morris & Dickson LLC (known to customers as M&D). M&D built their first 340B software solution over 10 years ago but at the time only made it available to M&D customers. M&D has a history of high customer satisfaction and customer loyalty. A key aspect of our satisfaction rates is developing tailored solutions that are forward thinking and open ended. These design aspects coupled with development processes that involve customer feedback enabled M&D to build the 340B Optimizer. The 340B Optimizer may be a newer solution in the national marketplace, but it is rooted in over ten years of development with the end user in mind.
