    Pharmacy Tools

    Our Contract Pharmacy Module is built for compliance and visibility. We give Covered Entities endless tools to audit compliance and determine savings captured in order to evaluate pharmacies in the contract pharmacy network.

    Evaluation Tools

    Clear visibility into savings generated at multiple levels. Clear visual into savings generated by each contract pharmacy with dispensing fees and admin fees factored in. Brand vs Generic capture rates as well savings reported per script, per NPI, per NDC, and per BIN (insurance plan). All of these tools assist covered entities in determining the successful launch of new contract pharmacies as well as evaluate existing contract pharmacies.

    High-Touch Client Relationships

    You will not be another ticket number with the 340B Optimizer. We have dedicated support sta that you can ask for by name. Your sales rep will stay with you throughout the entire partnership and consult whenever you need. We need you to be successful in order for us to be successful.

    Audit Ready

    Complete audit trail accountability is offered through extensive tracking and reporting features that record all transactions. Reports can be generated by patient ID (audits are patient driven) making it easy to run reports by patient ID number. Convenient and efficient ability to download transaction and eligibility data for any type of audit.

    Flexible True-Up Models

    Choose the appropriate True-Up model that is right for you. We have No True-Up models and traditional True-Models. We can tailor a True-Up model that is appropriate for your entity.
